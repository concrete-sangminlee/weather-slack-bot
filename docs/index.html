Features

🌡

Complete Weather Data

Temperature, humidity, pressure, wind, visibility, dew point, precipitation, cloud cover, UV index, solar radiation.

💨

Air Quality

US AQI, PM2.5, PM10, CO, NO2, O3 — with health advisories when levels are concerning.

📊

Smart Indices

Lifestyle score, discomfort index, laundry/car wash/food safety indices, health risk warnings.

👗

Outfit & Activities

11-tier clothing recommendation + weather-appropriate activity suggestions.

📈

Forecasts & Trends

6-hour hourly, 3-day outlook, 7-day trend chart, tomorrow change alerts.

🌍

Multi-City

Compare weather across up to 3 additional cities in a compact view.

🌈

Daylight & Moon

Sunrise/sunset progress bar, remaining daylight, current moon phase.

🌐

i18n Ready

Korean, English, Japanese locale files. Easy to add more languages.

⚙

Fully Configurable

Single config.yml — city, timezone, forecast days, display toggles, language.
