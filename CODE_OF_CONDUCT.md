# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, ethnicity, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that could be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to concrete.sangminlee@gmail.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
